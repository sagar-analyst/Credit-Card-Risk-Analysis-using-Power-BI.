## ğŸ¯ Business Problem
Credit card portfolios often face **concentration risk**, where a small proportion of customers contributes a large share of potential loss.

Without structured visibility into risk drivers, credit teams struggle to:

- Quantify exposure at risk

This project focuses on enabling **segment-driven credit risk decisions** instead of uniform policies.
